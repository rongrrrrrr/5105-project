PharmaESG

PharmaESG is a research-driven platform that analyzes ESG disclosures from pharmaceutical companies. Using advanced models and a customized scoring framework, we transform ESG reports into actionable insights. Our platform aims to help companies, investors, and stakeholders to navigate the future of sustainable healthcare.

About Us

Become best of you

We are building a specialized ESG evaluation platform tailored for the pharmaceutical industry. Using advanced NLP techniques and a custom scoring framework aligned with global standards, we analyze ESG reports to generate structured, interpretable ESG scores. Our system focuses on capturing both the exposure and management aspects of ESG performance across environmental, social, and governance pillars.

Our mission is to help investors, regulators, and stakeholders make informed decisions by turning ESG disclosures into meaningful, data-driven insights. !
